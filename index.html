Our Principles

We believe in building tools that embody these core values:

Accessibility First

The law belongs to everyone. Our tools should make legal documents accessible to anyone.

Roman Engineering

Build for longevity with solid foundations that will remain relevant for decades.

User-Driven Design

Keep the needs of legal professionals central to every design decision.

Open Source Philosophy

Develop transparent, community-friendly solutions that others can build upon.

Thoughtful Architecture

Prioritize correct, maintainable approaches over quick fixes.

Progressive Enhancement

Start with essential functionality and build additional capabilities in logical phases.

Development Roadmap

Building the future of legal tools with a thoughtful, phased approach

Phase 1: Core Navigation

Q2-Q3 2025

Building the foundation with multi-pane document viewing, basic citation detection, and core navigation features.

Phase 2: Enhanced Search

Q4 2025

Advanced search capabilities, visual heat maps, and improved citation intelligence.

Phase 3: Intelligent Citations

Q1-Q2 2026

Comprehensive citation detection, relationship mapping, and expanded document format support.

Phase 4: AI Assistance

Q3 2026 onwards

Contextual recommendations, document summarization, and advanced research assistance.
