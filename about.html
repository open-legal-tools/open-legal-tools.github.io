About Open Legal Tools

We're building open-source tools to make legal documents more accessible, navigable, and understandable for everyone.

Our Mission

The law belongs to everyone, but access to legal documents remains trapped behind paywalls or in static, difficult-to-navigate formats. Open Legal Tools was created to change that reality.

We believe that intelligent navigation, citation linking, and powerful search capabilities should be available to anyone who needs to understand legal documentsâ€”from judicial clerks to pro se litigants, from attorneys to members of the public.

By creating free, open-source tools with thoughtful design and robust functionality, we aim to make legal documents more accessible and understandable for everyone, regardless of technical skill or resources.

Our Core Values

Accessibility First

The law belongs to everyone. Our tools should make legal documents accessible to anyone.

Roman Engineering

Build for longevity with solid foundations that will remain relevant for decades.

User-Driven Design

Keep the needs of legal professionals central to every design decision.

Open Source Philosophy

Develop transparent, community-friendly solutions that others can build upon.

Thoughtful Architecture

Prioritize correct, maintainable approaches over quick fixes.

Progressive Enhancement

Start with essential functionality and build additional capabilities in logical phases.
